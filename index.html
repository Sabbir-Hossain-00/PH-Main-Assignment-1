Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving progress through creativity, technology, and smart solutions to transform Bangladesh's future sustainably

Sustainability

Ensuring a greener future by promoting renewable energy, eco-friendly practices, and responsible resource management.

Infrastructure

Building resilient, modern infrastructure with smart technologies to support sustainable growth and urban development.

Empowerment

Empowering communities through education, skills development, and equal opportunities for a prosperous and inclusive society.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future for Bangladesh.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Today, Bangladesh stands at a pivotal moment in its journey towards progress. While we have made remarkable strides in economic growth and development, challenges remain. From climate change to the rapid pace of urbanization, we must act swiftly and innovatively to secure a sustainable future. As Chief Advisor to Bangladesh 2.0, I urge everyone to join hands in this transformative effort. Our collective strength, through innovation, empowerment, and sustainable infrastructure, will guide us toward a prosperous and resilient nation. Let’s embrace this challenge and build a future where every Bangladeshi thrives.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
